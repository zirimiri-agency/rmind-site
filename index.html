Features

What You Can Do

✏️ Capture
Capture Anything

Tap a category, fill in the details, done. Add photos, locations, #hashtags, and notes to any entry.

📝 Lists
Lists & ChecklistsNew

Create grocery lists, packing lists, or any to-do. Check items off, reorder by dragging, and track progress with a visual bar.

⏰ Reminders
Smart Reminders

Set reminders on any entry. Repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or weekdays. Snooze with one swipe.

👥 Social
Friends & SharingNew

Add friends by email or invite code. Share entries like restaurant picks, shopping lists, or travel plans. They get their own copy to keep.

🗺️ Map
Map View

Every entry with a location appears on the map. Find that restaurant, that shop, that place you saved.

✨ Fields
Smart FieldsNew

Each category has tailored fields. Author for books, star ratings for restaurants, price for purchases, event dates, and more.

📱 Widgets
Home Screen Widgets

Small, medium, and large widgets. Capture a thought without even opening the app.

🔍 Search
Search & Filter

Filter by category, #hashtag, or reminders. Find anything instantly with powerful search.

Getting started

How It Works

1
Download

Get RMind free from the App Store. Sign in with Apple, Google, or email.

2
Capture

Tap a category, add a title and details, save. Add photos or a location if you want.

3
Organize

Use categories and #hashtags. Everything sorts itself.

4
Recall

Search, filter, or browse the map. Everything you saved is instantly findable.

5
Share

Add friends and share any entry as a copy. Or create a share link anyone can use.
